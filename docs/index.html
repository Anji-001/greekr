Beyond Reps.
See Inside the Movement.

A next-generation interactive mobile application that bridges the gap between theoretical anatomy and practical exercise execution using real-time, manipulatable 3D human models.

💡 Key Innovation

"Seeing what your muscles are doing in real time. Instead of just telling users what muscle is used, we visually demonstrate it using a live 3D model."

The Market Polarization Problem

Most fitness learners struggle to understand exact muscle engagement, differentiate between muscle heads (e.g., front vs. rear deltoid), and learn correct form.

Currently, the market is severely polarized. Traditional Anatomy apps are too theoretical. Traditional Fitness apps are too practical, lacking deep biomechanical explanations. AnatomyFit OS provides the missing bridge.

  • ✖ Lack of internal visualization
  • ✖ Confusion over primary vs stabilizer muscles
  • ✔ Our Solution: Real-time 3D highlighted engagement
